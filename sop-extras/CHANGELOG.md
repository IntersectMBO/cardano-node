## 0.3.0.0 -- 2025-04-03

### Breaking

- Refactor `Index` to use `NS ((:~:) x) xs`. Pattern synonyms `IZ` and `IS` are
  provided, making this change transparent for users. Some functions need
  additional constraints, hence the breaking nature of this change.
