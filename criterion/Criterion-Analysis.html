data OutlierEffect Source #

A description of the extent to which outliers in the sample data affect the sample mean and standard deviation.

Constructors

Unaffected

Less than 1% effect.

Slight

Between 1% and 10%.

Moderate

Between 10% and 50%.

Severe

Above 50% (i.e. measurements are useless).
